## Slide 3 — The problem

- ERPs (SAP, Oracle, Dynamics, Odoo) own **finance, inventory, procurement** — but
  are **weak, slow, and expensive** at **front-office + field execution**: CRM,
  van sales, routes, trade spend, approvals, real-time analytics.
- Businesses bolt on spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and point tools → **fragmented data,
  no real-time visibility, manual reconciliation**.
- **Ripping out the ERP is too risky and costly.** Replacing point tools with
  another monolith just moves the problem.
- In the **Arab/GCC market** specifically: weak Arabic-first UX, poor field-sales
  tooling, and integration projects that stall.

_Presenter: "The ERP isn't the enemy — the gap around it is."_

---

## Slide 4 — The solution

**VANTORA is a modular Business OS that fills the gap around the ERP.**

- **Adopt module-by-module** (Sales only / Field Ops only / Workflow only) up to
  the **full platform** — or a ready **industry pack**.
- **Coexists with the ERP**: the ERP stays the system of record for finance/
  inventory; VANTORA owns CRM, Sales, Field Ops, Trade Spend, Workflow, Analytics
  — **syncing only the entities you choose, in the direction you choose**.
- **One shared core**, multi-tenant, RLS-secured, Arabic/English + RTL, premium UX.
- **Low switching risk** → fast time-to-value → land-and-expand.

_Presenter: this is the thesis slide — modular + coexistence = low-risk adoption._

## Slide 12 — Use case: FMCG Distribution

- **Setup:** wholesale/delivery tenant; Sales, Inventory, CRM, Analytics, **Field
  Ops**, Workflow + Distribution vertical.
- **Coexistence:** ERP owns materials/stock/finance → **sync in** customers,
  products, stock; VANTORA reps run routes/journey/van sales → **sync out** orders
  + trade-spend settlements.
- **Daily loop:** journey plan → visit → van sale (tiered) → collection → daily
  settlement → orders flow to the ERP; managers see routes, sales, collection,
  stock risk **in real time**.
- **Win:** higher visit compliance, accurate orders in the ERP, live collection &
  stock visibility. _(Demo: seeded FMCG environment.)_

---

## Slide 13 — Use case: Electrical Retail & Wholesale

- **Setup:** electronics tenant; Sales, Inventory, Purchasing, Finance, POS,
  Analytics + **Electrical pack**.
- **Multi-tier pricing:** Retail / Semi-wholesale / Wholesale / **Project** (with a
  negotiated per-line floor) — enforced automatically by customer tier.
- **Serials + warranty + RMA:** serialized high-value items tracked unit-by-unit;
  warranties auto-created on sale; **RMA** handles repair / replace / refund and
  orchestrates returns + accounting.
- **Supplier + customer returns:** defective-to-vendor and customer returns post
  stock + journals correctly.
- **Coexistence:** items/stock/customers in; sales/invoices out — pricing,
  warranty, serials, RMA stay VANTORA-owned, never overwritten. _(Pack is
  production-live — demo-ready today.)_
